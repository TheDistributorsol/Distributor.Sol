About

The Distributor is a partner memecoin designed to support the Distribute ecosystem. With every transfer, 10% tax is applied to fuel buybacks, burns and rewards. It's not just a token—it's an engine for growth.

Tokenomics

  • 10% Transfer Tax
  • 30% goes to the dev wallet (marketing, buybacks, burns)
  • 70% goes to Distribute for buybacks and burns or is used for rewards

Why Hold?

As a holder, you benefit from memecoin-style gains while directly boosting Distribute’s economy. It’s a win-win for everyone in the ecosystem.
